other_events

Table containing events.Intended for curvature and gradient events. Events from junctions and crossings should be in jc rather than here.

sec = section label. Must exist in network.

geom = linestring geometry.

reversed=True where in direction of section.

category=category of piece

s_ch=start chainage of event. In direction of section.

e_ch=end chainage of event. In direction of section.
